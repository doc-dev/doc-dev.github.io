PRIVACY POLICY - PURSUANT TO ART. 13 OF THE GENERAL DATA PROTECTION REGULATION - EU REGULATION 679/2016

 

Dear Participant,

We provide you the following information on the processing of your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (EU Regulation 679/2016).

 

CONTACTS

The Data Controller is Politecnico di Torino, represented by the Rector, with statutory seat in Corso Duca degli Abruzzi, 24 – 10129 Torino.

You can contact the Data Controller at: politecnicoditorino@pec.polito.it (certified e-mail)

For further information and inquiries write to: privacy@polito.it

You can contact the Data Protection Officer (DPO) of Politecnico di Torino for inquiries on the processing of your personal data and your rights at: dpo@polito.it ; dpo@pec.polito.it (certified e-mail)

 

PRICIPLES, LEGAL BASIS AND PURPOSES OF THE PROCESSING OF PERSONAL DATA

Politecnico di Torino (Data Controller) will process your personal data in accordance with the principles of data minimization, accuracy, storage limitation, integrity, confidentiality and accountability (article 5, paragraph 1 of the GDPR) and pursuant to art. 6, paragraph 1, letter e) of the GDPR (“processing is necessary for the performance of a task carried out in the public interest or in the exercise of official authority vested in the Controller”). When your consent is required, your personal data will be processed in accordance with art. 6, paragraph 1, letter a) of the GDPR (the data subject has given consent to the processing of his or her personal data for one or more specific purposes).

Politecnico di Torino will process the data you provided in electronic format for the purposes of  research in the field of social interaction, regarding the research projects: “Hacking a complex world: unraveling the machanism underlying complex social and technological phenomena”, “Macro to Micro: uncovering the hidden mechanisms driving network dynamics” (Mac2Mic), and “Network-based Modeling of Infectious Disease Epidemics in a Mobile Population: Strengthening Preparedness and Containment”.

The data that will be gathered and studyed, after anonymisation, will be: Google account, if explicitly choosen, Bluetooth sensors, WiFi sensors, approximate geospatial position, on/off state of the screen.

 

Your specific consent will be given upon installation of the Android Application on your device in a designated procedure.

 

TRANFER OF PERSONAL DATA

Your personal data can be disclosed by Politecnico di Torino, and are in any case visible, to its staff members and collaborators who will receive appropriate training from the Data Controller.

Politecnico di Torino is allowed to disclose your data to third parties which cooperate with the University  for research purposes linked to the projects: “Hacking a complex world: unraveling the machanism underlying complex social and technological phenomena”, “Macro to Micro: uncovering the hidden mechanisms driving network dynamics” (Mac2Mic), and “Network-based Modeling of Infectious Disease Epidemics in a Mobile Population: Strengthening Preparedness and Containment”.

Personal data are stored in the university servers (located inside Politecnico) and/or in external servers which belong to providers of technical and administrative services.

Your personal data will not be transferred to non-EU countries (third-party countries) unless adequately anonymised.

 

PERIOD FOR WHICH PERSONAL DATA ARE STORED

Personal data for which your consent has been acquired are kept for no longer than is necessary for the purposes of the research projects.

 

NECESSITY OF PROVIDING PERSONAL DATA

Providing access to your personal data is non-compulsory. The participation to the research project is not possible unless access to personal data is given.

 

RIGHTS OF THE DATA SUBJECT

You are the Data Subject and you have the right to obtain from the Data Controller (in accordance with articles 15, 16, 17, 18, 19, 20 and 21 of the GDPR):

·       access to your personal data and to the information referred to in art. 15 of the GDPR;

·       rectification of inaccurate personal data (including the right to have incomplete personal data completed);

·       erasure of your personal data (except for data which must be kept by Politecnico di Torino) unless there are overriding legitimate grounds for the processing;

·       restriction of processing pursuant to art. 18 of the GDPR.

You also have the right to:

·       withdraw your consent given for non-compulsory processing of personal data, without affecting the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal;

·       data portability.

 

Please contact the Data Controller if you want to exercise your rights.

 

COMPLAINT

You have the right to contact the Italian Data Protection Authority (Garante per la protezione dei dati personali) following the instructions available at: https://www.garanteprivacy.it/web/guest/home/docweb/-/docweb-display/docweb/4535524
